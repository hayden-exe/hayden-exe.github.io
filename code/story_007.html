"I don't want to alarm you too bad, but..."

Mai's voice catches your attention as she finishes her task.

"It's about to get really messy." Her smile does little to comfort your unease.

Kyle puts his hand on your arm and pulls you over to where he's standing by the far wall. There's a small circle of candles around his spot which you stand outside of.

"I trust you, okay?"

Your growing anxiety does not let up.

Tai is drawing a smaller chalk circle around Jason, connecting his shape to the main circle with a thick line. Six more thick lines branch from the sides of the circle, making it look like some kind of abstract spider.

The twin next to you leans over and says, "You should close your eyes."

The way she says it makes your whole body feel chilled.

"That is, unless you'd rather switch places with him..."



No way! Try to leave again.

No, thanks. You close your eyes.

Sure, why not? Take his place.
