You decide that this is too much and you're not the type of person to trespass in the middle of the night.

"Wait, are you sure?" Kyle sounds disappointed but he doesn't look like he's going to argue with you.

With a nod, you wave goodbye and start walking back the way you two came: a long dirt path that disappears into a line of trees. You pass a car full of the other members of the friend group, but they don't stop for you.

It's past 3 AM when you get to your bed. You pass out.

You hear the news of a mysterious group suicide out where Kyle had invited you last night. 3 bodies were found and 4 are missing.

If you're lucky, you'll forget this ever happened.

Otherwise, the possible outcomes of that night will plague you for years to come.
