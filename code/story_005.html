Silence settles. Their chattering stops and the vague sound of insects can be heard outside. It feels very still, almost like a liminal space.

Candles flicker to life and the dim light leaves a soft glow around them.

Mai and Tai pull out containers of salt and start at the closest point of the circle and split as they cover the chalk with a thick line of the salt.

Kyle's busy finding the right page to start on in his book.

Gregory, Chasity, and Shawni are standing off to the side and watching.

When they're done with the salt, the twins fetch chalk from their pockets and start etching more symbols along the outside of the circle.
